Vegetarian Nachos

Ingredients:

  • 1 sliced yellow onion
  • 1 sliced bell pepper
  • 1 can of black beans
  • 1 cup of diced tomatoes
  • Cabot cheddar cheese
  • diced avocado
  • cilantro
  • 1 bag of tortilla chips

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven at 400 degrees.
  2. Heat a large pan with cooking spray or oil over medium heat for a minute.
  3. Add onions and peppers and sautee until tender for 5 minutes.
  4. Turn off the heat.
  5. Add chips to a baking sheet.
  6. Add beans and sprinkled cheese on top.
  7. Bake it in the oven for about 12 minutes until the cheese melts.
  8. Serve immediately.

Loaded Vegetarian Nachos

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of rinsed lentils
  • 2 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of pepper
  • 1 cup of shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup of diced tomato
  • 1/3 cup of fresh, chopped cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 lb of tortilla chips

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit (175 degrees Celsius).
  2. Boil the lentils, broth, salt, pepper, in a large pot. Simmer after boiling and cook for 35 minutes.
  3. On a baking sheet, add a layer of tortilla chips along with lentils and cheese.
  4. Bake until the cheese has melted.
  5. Add lettuce, tomatoes, cilantro, and oregano to the top. Enjoy!
