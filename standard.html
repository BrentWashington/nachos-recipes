Simple Nachos

Tortilla chips covered with ground beef, black beans, and shredded cheese. This simple yet tasty recipe is perfect for parties or simply relaxing, and it only takes 30 minutes of prepping and cooking. Feel free to branch off and add your own flair during the process!

Ingredients:

  • Tortilla chips
  • Ground beef
  • Black beans
  • Cheddar cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven at 375 degrees.
  2. Place tortilla chips on the baking sheet. Make sure to spread them out into an even layer. Set aside the sheet for later.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add chopped cheef and cook (making sure they are crumbling) for six minutes.
  4. Stir in the black beans, spoon the beef mixture over the chips, and sprinkle the cheese over the beef layer.
  5. Cook in a preheated oven until the cheese is melted for about seven minutes.
  6. Serve immediately.

Beef Nachos

Do you need more beef in your nachos? Do the nachos your favorite restaurant serves not fulfill your high expectations? Have no fear, I have you covered. Behold, the Beef Nachos Supreme! These are the perfect nachos for any occasion. A non-formal one that is. You will have to set aside 15 minutes for preparation and 15 minutes for cooking.

Ingredients:

  • Taco seasoning
  • One pound of ground beef
  • One half of diced yellow onion
  • Two bags of tortilla chips
  • 16 ounces of shredded cheese (personal choice)
  • Two sliced green onions

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven at 400 degrees.
  2. Cook the ground beef and diced onions over medium-high heat for about eight minutes until fully cooked and drain.
  3. Sprinkle the taco seasoning on the beef in the pan.
  4. Stir in 3/4 cup of water, simmer, reduce heat, and simmer again until the liquid evaporates.
  5. Add chips to two baking sheets and cover each with a layer of ground beef.
  6. Sprinkle shredded cheese on the pans and bake them at 400 degrees for 15 minutes.
  7. Remove the pans from the oven and sprinkle the diced tomatoes and green onions on top.
  8. Serve immediately.
