Ultimate Vegan Nachos

The perfect game day food or dish that will suit everyone. The preparation time for this recipe is 15 minutes and it will take 15 minutes to cook. The total time is an 30 minutes and serves six, so take that into consideration when preparing for a large social gathering.

Ingredients:

  • Taco Meat
  • Vegan Cheese Sauce
  • 1 Bag of Tortilla Chips
  • 1 15-oz Canned Black Beans
  • 2 diced Roma Tomatoes
  • Fresh Jalapenos
  • Cashew Sour Cream
  • Chopped Green Onions
  • Salsa
  • Diced Avocados/Guacamole
  • Chopped Cilantro

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven at 400 degrees.
  2. Drain and rinse the black beans.
  3. Place the tortilla chips on a baking sheet along with the ingredients (in any order).
  4. Place in the oven and cook at 400 degrees for about 15 minutes.
  5. Serve immediately after finished.

Cheesy Vegan Nachos

This recipe paired with queso will be one of the most delicious vegan nachos you will ever taste! It takes 30 minutes to prepare and serves four. It's also gluten-free!

Ingredients:

  • 8 Cups of Tortilla Chips
  • 1 Batch of Vegan Queso
  • 1 15-ounce Can of drained Black Beans
  • 1 Cup of Guacamole
  • 1 Cup of Chunky Salsa

Steps:

  1. Prepare the vegan queso and heat up the black beans (with seasoning if desired).
  2. Mash the avocado with lime juice and sea salt to prepare the guacamole.
  3. Arrange chips on a serving platter (large or individual) and add toppings in any order your prefer.
  4. Enjoy immediately!
